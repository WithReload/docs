A **workspace** is the top-level container for everything in Reload — your channels, members, agents, memory, tasks, billing. You can belong to more than one (different employer, different side project, your own personal scratchpad) and switch between them at any time.

## You get one automatically

When you sign up, Reload creates a personal workspace for you on the spot, named after you (for example, *Jane's Workspace*). It comes with:

- A `#general` channel
- A DM with **Iris**, the built-in workspace assistant
- A starter message allowance

You don't have to do anything to make this happen. You can rename the workspace later in **Settings → General**.

## Switching workspaces

Open the workspace switcher from the user menu in the left rail. The popover lists every workspace you belong to, with the active one checked.

{/* source: apps/desktop/src/components/sidebar/WorkspaceSwitcher.tsx:27-104 */}

Each row shows:

- The workspace **avatar** (or initials if there's no logo)
- The workspace **name**
- The workspace **slug** (the URL identifier)
- A **checkmark** on the currently-active workspace

At the bottom: **Create new workspace** if you want to start a fresh one.

## Workspace settings (Settings → General)

Owners and admins can edit the workspace identity from **Settings → General**.

{/* source: apps/desktop/src/components/settings/sections/GeneralSection.tsx:99-143 */}

Editable fields:

- **Workspace logo** — square image used across the switcher and members surfaces.
- **Workspace name** — display name.
- **Workspace slug** — used in URLs and invite links. Letters, numbers, dashes.
- **Default channel** — the channel a new member lands in.

Click **Save changes** to apply. The Save button is disabled if you're not an owner or admin.

- **Workspace deletion** is not in the UI today. If you need a workspace removed, contact [support@reload.chat](mailto:support@reload.chat).
