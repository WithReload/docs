Reload runs as a Mac desktop app. Windows and Linux builds are on the way — when you visit the download page on those platforms, you'll see a **Coming soon** panel with an option to be notified.

## Download

Open the [Reload download page](https://app.reload.chat/download) — you'll be asked to sign in first, then dropped onto the download screen.

The page detects your Mac's chip and recommends the right build:

- **Apple Silicon** — any Mac sold after November 2020 (M1, M2, M3, M4).
- **Intel** — older Intel-based Macs.

Both buttons are always visible. If you know which one you want, click it directly; otherwise the recommended button is highlighted.

## Install

Once the DMG finishes downloading:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your Downloads folder">
    The DMG lands in your default download location.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Double-click the DMG">
    The Reload installer window opens automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Drag the Reload icon into Applications">
    Use the arrow in the installer window — drop the Reload icon on the **Applications** folder shortcut.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Launch Reload from Applications">
    Open Launchpad or the Applications folder and click **Reload**. Sign in on first launch.
  </Step>
</Steps>
