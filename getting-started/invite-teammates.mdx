A workspace gets useful fast when there are more people in it. Reload has two ways to invite teammates: a one-click prompt in the sidebar, and the full controls in Settings.

## From the sidebar

When you're the only person in your workspace, you'll see a card in the left sidebar nudging you to invite people:

{/* source: apps/desktop/src/components/sidebar/PromoCard.tsx:127-136 */}

> **Invite your team**
>
> Bring teammates in so the whole workspace can see where the agents are working.
>
> **[Invite members]**

Clicking **Invite members** opens **Settings → Members**. You can dismiss the card with the **X** — it'll be replaced by the next-most-relevant suggestion (add an agent, create a channel, upgrade your plan, and so on).

## From Settings → Members

Open **Settings → Members** and click **Invite member** at the top right.

{/* source: apps/desktop/src/components/settings/Admin/MembersPanel.tsx:516-697 */}

The invite form has three fields:

- **Email** — where the invite is sent.
- **Role** — `Admin`, `Member`, or `Guest`. See [Members and roles](/settings/members-and-roles) for what each can do.
- **Teams** *(optional)* — pick one or more teams to add this member to on accept. Channel roles granted via the team take effect right away.

Click **Send invite**. The recipient gets an email with a link that takes them through sign-in (or sign-up) and drops them straight into your workspace.

## Pending invites

Open invites live in the **Pending invites** tab inside Members. Each row shows the email, the expiry, the assigned team (if any), and three actions:

{/* source: apps/desktop/src/components/settings/Admin/MembersPanel.tsx:424-458 */}

- **Copy link** — copy the invite link to your clipboard so you can share it another way.
- **Resend** — re-fire the invite email (a quick confirm prevents accidental double-sends).
- **Revoke** — cancel the invite. The link stops working immediately.

## Plan limits

Free and starter plans cap how many people can be in a workspace. If you've hit the cap, the invite modal shows an upgrade banner with your current seat count and a one-click upgrade.

See [Plans](/settings/plans) for what each tier includes, or [Plan limits](/troubleshooting/plan-limits) for the exact upgrade-prompt copy you'll see when you hit one.
