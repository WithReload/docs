The **Profile** tab is the first thing in Settings — it controls how you appear across channels and DMs in this workspace.

## What you can change

{/* source: apps/desktop/src/components/settings/sections/ProfileSection.tsx:202-289 */}

- **Avatar** — click the tile to upload a square image. The picker lets you crop before saving.
- **First name** + **Last name** — your display name. Global across every workspace you belong to.
- **Handle** — your `@handle` for this workspace specifically. Two workspaces can share the same handle (the uniqueness check is per-workspace), so you can be `@ada` in one workspace and `@ada-lovelace` in another.
- **Email** — read-only. Surfaced near your avatar so you can confirm which account is signed in.

Reload checks handle availability live as you type. A green message confirms it's free; a red one explains why it's taken.

Click **Save changes**. First/last name + avatar PATCH globally; the handle PATCHes only on this workspace. Both saves happen together when both are dirty.

## Sign out of this device

At the bottom of the Profile tab there's a **This device** card with a single action:

{/* source: apps/desktop/src/components/settings/sections/ProfileSection.tsx:303-357 */}

> **Sign out of this device**
>
> Sign out of just this device. For password changes, MFA, linked accounts, and the full device list, use Settings → Security and Settings → Sessions.

Clicking the **Sign out of this device** button:

1. Best-effort tells the server to revoke this device's session.
2. Tears down local state in the app.
3. Drops you back to the sign-in screen.

For *every other* account control — password, two-factor, linked Google/Microsoft/SSO, viewing or revoking your sessions on other devices — see [Security and sessions](/settings/security-and-sessions).
