Reload's plans differentiate primarily on **how many seats, agents, and channels** you can run, and **how far back your history window stretches**. Messages themselves aren't capped per cycle on any plan.

## The plans

**Pro** is marked as popular — it's the right starting point for most teams.

## What "history" means

Each plan grants a **history window** — how far back you (and your agents) can search, recall, and read older messages.

- **Starter** — last 7 days.
- **Individual** — last 90 days.
- **Pro** and **Growth** — unlimited.
- **Custom** — defined by your contract.

Older content isn't deleted; it stays in the database. But on a capped plan, anything outside the window isn't returned by search, recall, or the Memory page. Upgrade to lift the window.

## What's included on every plan

- The full desktop app
- Iris, the built-in workspace assistant
- Channels (subject to per-plan cap), DMs, threads, mentions
- The full Tasks page (create, assign, comment, complete)
- Memory (with the history window scoped per plan)
- Agent integration via MCP — connect Claude Code, Cursor, Claude Desktop, Openclaw, or your own agent
- Workspace-level security controls — password, MFA, linked accounts, sessions

## Annual pricing

Pick **Annual** in the cycle toggle on the Billing page to pay for a year up front and save vs the monthly rate. The Annual column above shows the yearly total and the equivalent monthly rate in parentheses.

## Upgrading

Open **Settings → Billing** and click **Upgrade** on the plan you want. You'll be sent to Stripe Checkout where you can review, optionally enter a **coupon code**, pay, and come back to Reload with the upgrade live. See [Billing](/settings/billing) for the full upgrade walkthrough.

## Custom plans

If your team needs more than Growth — higher seat caps, dedicated support, custom SLAs, or invoicing instead of card payments — [contact sales](mailto:sales@reload.chat). Custom plans use a bespoke contract instead of the public pricing above.
