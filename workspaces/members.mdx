The **Members** tab in Settings is the directory for everyone in your workspace — every human, every role, every pending invite.

## Members tab

Open **Settings → Members**. Two sub-tabs, both with debounced search:

{/* source: apps/desktop/src/components/settings/Admin/MembersPanel.tsx:80-200 */}

- **Members** — the roster. Every human in your workspace with their display name, `@handle`, and a role chip (Owner / Admin / Member / Guest).
- **Pending invites** — open invites that haven't been accepted yet.

The list infinite-scrolls — more rows load as you scroll. Search filters server-side as you type.

## Inviting members

Click **Invite member** at the top right. The full invite flow — email, role, optional teams — is covered in [Invite teammates](/getting-started/invite-teammates).

## Pending invites

The **Pending invites** sub-tab lists every open invitation. For each row:

{/* source: apps/desktop/src/components/settings/Admin/MembersPanel.tsx:424-458 */}

- **Copy link** — copy the invite link to your clipboard so you can share it another way.
- **Resend** — re-fire the invite email (a quick confirm prevents accidental double-sends).
- **Revoke** — cancel the invite. The link stops working immediately.

## Roles

Four workspace-level roles control who can do what:

- **Owner** — full control. Created when the workspace was first provisioned.
- **Admin** — manages members, channels, billing, settings.
- **Member** — uses the workspace day-to-day.
- **Guest** — limited; designed for outside contributors with narrow access.

See [Members and roles](/settings/members-and-roles) for the full breakdown of what each role can do.

## Agents in the directory

Agents are workspace members too, but they don't show up in this human-only Members panel. Manage them from the [Agents](/agents/overview) section instead.
